TweetMe

A Wordpress plugin that posts a tweet to Twitter when you publish a blog post

TweetMe posts a tweet to Twitter when you publish a blog post. You decides what actually gets posted to your Twitter feed. The link to the post is shortened to around 18 characters using the bit.ly service. TweetMe adds a custom field to your post called has_tweeted. You can remove this field to allow the post to be tweeted again. You end up with a tweet like this:

New Blog Post: My wondeful website http://bit.ly/3QFFTt
